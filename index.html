Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Our SIL services help build independence, develop life skills, and provide the right level of assistance for everyday living. Participants can live in shared or individual accommodation with daily support from trained staff.

Learn More

Respite & Short Stays

Our respite services provide temporary support in a safe, comfortable environment. Participants can recharge, explore new activities, and enjoy social opportunities.

Emergency Accommodation

We offer urgent, short-term crisis accommodation for participants experiencing unexpected situations that impact their wellbeing or safety.

Group Activities

We provide recreational, social, and skill-building sessions that help individuals build confidence while forming meaningful community connections.

Community Participation

We empower individuals to take part in everyday community life — exploring hobbies, building social skills, discovering new interests, and engaging in inclusive activities in safe and supportive environments.

Mentoring Services

We provide person-centred mentoring support that helps participants develop skills, strengthen independence, and achieve their personal goals. Our mentors offer guidance through life challenges, provide positive role modelling, and build confidence to support meaningful participation in the community. We offer mentoring for children and young people, adults, and older persons with disability, including individuals with psychosocial disabilities, those experiencing social isolation, and participants working towards greater independence and community involvement.
